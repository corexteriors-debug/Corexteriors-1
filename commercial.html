Who We Are

Core Exteriors is an independently owned exterior maintenance company based in London, Ontario. We have grown into a full-service commercial and residential exterior maintenance company serving property managers, developers, and building owners across Southern Ontario.

We operate with no franchise fees and no corporate overhead. That means competitive pricing without cutting corners on quality or coverage.

We operate a fleet of 6+ fully equipped trucks and trailers. With over 10 crew members, and 20+ people working across projects daily, we have the people and equipment to handle your property without delays.

How We Work

Straightforward process, professional bidding.

Flexible Service Models

On-Call Service: Same day or next day availability. You call, we show up. Perfect for one-off jobs, emergencies, or irregular maintenance. No long-term commitment required.

Contract Service: Scheduled recurring maintenance. Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or seasonal. One point of contact, consistent crew, predictable pricing.

Transparent Bidding & Billing

Everything starts with a free on-site walkthrough. You receive a detailed written quote within 48 hours, fully itemized with no hidden fees.

We understand commercial work runs on a bid system. When you send us a scope, we provide a thorough, professional bid with proof of insurance so we can be the easy choice. Payment terms are Net 30.
